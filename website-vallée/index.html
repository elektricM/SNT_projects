Welcome to ESP32 & ESP8266 Website

This website provides information about ESP32 and ESP8266 development boards.

ESP32

The ESP32 is a powerful yet low-cost microcontroller with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities.

ESP8266

The ESP8266 is a low-cost Wi-Fi microchip with full TCP/IP stack and microcontroller capability.
